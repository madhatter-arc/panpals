Remember when the internet felt fun and honest and you could share small wholesome pieces of your day without thinking twice? PanPals is bringing that feeling back for whatever you cook. We decided to build ONE BIG TABLE for every kind of cooking. Not to collect perfect recipes or just plan meals, not to track calories or optimise yet another part of life. We wanted a place that simply celebrates real people in real kitchens making real food.

This is our attempt to bring people back to that table. If you want to sit with us pull up a chair and show us your plate and we'll show you ours.
